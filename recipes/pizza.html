This is a classic pizza that is very tasty

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • parmesan
  • mozarella
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • tomato sauce
  • salt & pepper
  • sugar
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. First preheat the oven to highest temperature 295C
  2. Make the sauce, heat olive oil and saute the garlic for 30 seconds. Then add tomato sauce, oregano, sugar, salt and pepper. Let it cook for 5 minutes
  3. Next you need to shape the dough
  4. Spread the pizza sauce on the pizza and put on parmesan, mozarella and pepperoni
  5. Last just bake it in the oven
